Nitte Open Source Conference 2016

To spread open source awareness in engineering colleges in and around
Udupi & Mangalore, India and to inspire students to contribute to the open source movement.
Students will have an oppurtunity to learn from and interact with members
of different open source communities. It is also a platform for the students to
share new innovative ideas/concepts with community members.

The format of the conference will be as follows,

Presentations

40 mins presentations from 11 speakers from different communities on any of the topics listed below.
Plus 10 mins of Q&A for each presentation.

Lightening Talks

There are 8 slots, each of 12 mins, available for the student audiance to present their
cool ideas/concepts/demos etc. Plus 3 mins of Q&A for each lightening talk.
The topic may be anything, either from the list below OR anything that you think is cool to share.
And at the end of NOS Conf 2016 there will be a prize for 1st and 2nd best lightening talk!.

Submit your lightening talk before Feb 29th, 2016
